Language Transfer Analysis

Voiced and voiceless consonants
  • These do not match up neatly – t/d, p/b, k/g all sound different to their Vietnamese equivalents. It can thus be difficult for learners to produce the correct voiced variants (Vietnamese versions are strongly implosive). Further, p (the unvoiced plosive) does not exist in Vietnamese 1 - page
