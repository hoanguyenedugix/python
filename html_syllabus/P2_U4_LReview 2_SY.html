Lesson Outcomes

Students can read and understand a text containing the units' target language.

Communication

Support Strategies:

  • (Reading) Read the review text aloud as a class. Ask: “Can you find a [vocab/grammar point]?” Students highlight or underline examples in their books.

Stretch Strategies:

  • “Draw and Check” Dictation – Read 4–6 short sentences using target language (e.g., “There’s a red car under the bed.”). Students draw each scene. Afterwards, show or read again so they can self-check and explain what they heard.

Students can listen and identify the units' target language correctly.
